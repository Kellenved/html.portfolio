The Best TV-Series According to Nick

My top 3 all-time favorite TV-Series.

The 3-Body Problem

This is a series made after a verry good book. The sci-fi elements are very well done.

One Piece

This is a very good anime, the theme behind it being that you need to get out there and explore the world making friends along the way.

Being Human

It is a good police sci-fi series, the ideea that in the future there is a robot made that is so human-like but it's considered a defective product has a big impact.
